Getting Started

  • Enable location permissions for real-time friend tracking and map accuracy
  • Turn on “Share my location” to appear on the live festival map
  • Add friends using your unique friend code, or “Bump to connect” in person
  • Star artists to build your personal schedule — My Day follows the current festival day automatically; tap a day to browse another one
  • On first open, Festi picks the festival closest to today’s date — switch anytime in Settings, where each festival shows its dates

Location & Battery

  • Pick an accuracy/battery profile in Account → Accuracy & battery — Low, Balanced, or High
  • When your battery drops below ~20%, Festi automatically switches to Low accuracy to save power (and back once you charge); a notice in Account shows when this happens
  • “Only when I’m at the festival” (on by default) pauses sharing when you’re more than ~1 km from a venue — friends won’t see you at your hotel
  • Friends’ pins show a direction arrow while they’re walking, and a soft circle when their GPS fix is coarse — that circle is the position uncertainty, not a “range”

Common Issues

  • Location not updating: ensure “Always Allow” location access is enabled
  • Friends can’t see me: confirm location sharing is enabled and the friendship is accepted. If “Only when I’m at the festival” is on (it’s the default), you only appear within ~1 km of a venue — turn it off in Account to share anywhere
  • No “friend is at my stage” alerts: the “Alert when a friend joins my stage” toggle must be on, on both phones — the receiver’s toggle decides whether they get the push. Also check system notification permissions for Festi
  • Missing notifications: check system notification permissions for Festi
  • A friend request looks stuck on “pending”: open the Friends tab (or bring the app back to the foreground) — it re-syncs with the server each time
  • Map not loading: restart the app or verify your network connection

Account & Data

You can fully manage your data inside the app:

Account → Privacy & data → Delete account & data

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, friends, shared location, activity stats, and any blocks or reports you created. A fresh, empty account is created so you can keep using the app.
